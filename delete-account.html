Delete Account

Last Updated: January 2025

Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read this page carefully before proceeding.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you request account deletion, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account information (email, display name, user ID)
  • Your game data (scores, achievements, statistics)
  • Your game history and progress
  • All data associated with your account in our systems

Warning: This action cannot be reversed. Once your account is deleted, you will lose:

  • All achievements and trophies
  • Your all-time score and leaderboard position
  • Your game progress and statistics
  • Access to any in-app purchases or power refills

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account, please contact us using one of the following methods:

Email us at:

tophundred.contact@gmail.com

Please include the following information in your request:

  • Your email address associated with the account (or display name if you used anonymous sign-in)
  • A clear statement that you want to delete your account
  • Confirmation that you understand the deletion is permanent

Processing Time

We will process your account deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Data Retention

After account deletion, we may retain certain information for a limited period as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

  • Records required for tax or legal compliance
  • Information necessary to prevent fraud or abuse
  • Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be linked to your account

Third-Party Services

Please note that some data may be retained by third-party services we use:

  • Google Firebase: Account deletion requests will be processed according to Firebase's data retention policies.
  • Google AdMob: Ad-related data may be retained according to AdMob's privacy policies.
  • App Stores: Purchase history may be retained by Google Play Store or Apple App Store according to their policies.

Alternative: Delete Account from App

If you prefer, you can also request account deletion directly from within the app:

  1. Open the Top Hundred app
  2. Go to Settings (if available) or contact support through the app
  3. Request account deletion

Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us at:

tophundred.contact@gmail.com
